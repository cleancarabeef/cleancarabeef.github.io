Asda is selling mince pie filled cookies and they look like the perfect Christmas treat

  • Post author By admin
  • Post date November 10, 2019

WHAT better way to get in the Festive spirit that with a mince pie filled cookie?

If you think that sounds delicious, head to Asda where you can pick up four for the bargain price of £1.

The soft cookies are filled with mincemeat and topped with a sweet dusting, perfect for kicking off your Christmas celebrations.

They’re an Asda bakery product and they’re not available online so if you want to try them you’ll need to pick them up in store.

You can find your nearest Asda using the store locator.

The cookies were spotted by eagle-eyed Instagram user NewFoodsUK in the Tamworth Asda.

The Sun has asked Asda to confirm if the treats are available nationwide and will update this story once we know.

We also asked for a nutritional breakdown of the cookies.

The Instagram post got almost 3,000 likes in a day.

KevSnackReviews commented: “Want those cookies.”

MrsRichx added: “You’ll never look back!! I’ll get some for us.”

And Natalie Cahill added: “Oh my word those cookies.”

Those aren’t the only mince pie cookies available at Asda.

They also have eight extra special mince pie cookies in their grocery section.

These aren’t freshly baked like the new ones, and they cost £1.50 for the pack.

These cookies are infused with French brandy and crafted with an all-butter recipe.

Tesco also have a grocery option – but it’s more expensive.

You can a tin of Tesco Finest mince pie filled cookies for £4. Each tin contains eight cookies.

Each biscuit is filled  with rich, fruity mincemeat inspired jam and finished with a light dusting of fine sugar.

We couldn’t find any other mince pie stuffed cookies available online, but there are lots of recipes if you want to make your own.
